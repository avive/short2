Team Human Performance

A new-media art that happens simultaneously in the physical world and the virtual world on the blockchain.

Created and performed to manifest the spirit of the new era of decentralized art. The work will be both created and presented in a performance art events in a workshop like settings. Participants take part in the Team Human project as it is dynamically takes form on the blockchain and in the session’s space. They will learn about the operative tools and potential of tomorrow’s art world. We will explore together how the blockchain will transform the art world and will provide an opportunity for participants to take part of in the creation of a blockchain art piece.
